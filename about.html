OUR STORY

Wellness, intentionally.

EVERY CHOICE
HAS A PURPOSE

Base Wellness Cafe was born from a simple belief — that what you put into your body should make you feel as good as it tastes. Tucked into Roehampton High Street in SW15, we're a luxury wellness café built for people who take their wellbeing seriously, without taking the fun out of it.

Every item on our menu is crafted with intention. From ceremonial grade matcha to collagen-enriched functional smoothies and nourishing brunch plates, everything we serve is designed to support your skin, your energy and your everyday life.
